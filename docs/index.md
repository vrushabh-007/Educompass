# Welcome to Educompass

Educompass is an AI-powered college search and counseling platform built with Next.js, Supabase, and Google Genkit.

## Overview

Finding the right university can be challenging. Educompass aims to simplify this journey by combining search capabilities with AI-driven insights to help students find their best-fit colleges, draft statements of purpose, and connect with alumni.

## Key Features

- **College Search Engine:** Filter and discover global universities based on rankings, locations, acceptance rates, and required exams.
- **AI Recommendations:** Get personalized college suggestions tailored to your academic profile and preferences using context-aware AI.
- **AI Counselor:** Chat with an interactive bot equipped with RAG (Retrieval-Augmented Generation) to answer complex questions about admissions, funding, and campus life.
- **SOP & Essay Assistant:** Draft and refine your Statement of Purpose, receiving structural and content feedback generated by advanced LLM flows.
- **Alumni Network Directory:** Explore notable alumni across different institutions for inspiration and networking.

## Target Audience

- **High School Students:** Looking for undergraduate programs and advice.
- **Graduates:** Searching for Master's or PhD opportunities globally.
- **Parents & Counselors:** Seeking reliable data and tools to support their students.
